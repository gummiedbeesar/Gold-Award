Get Started with the Research Tool

I created this website so that students can have easy access to resources that could be helpful throughout the college research and application process. Based on some research and personal experiences I have created documents and compiled as much helpful information as I could.
However, I cannot review applications/essays or guarentee acceptance into any university because I am not qualified to do so. The purpose of this website is to provide resources that can reduce stress during the college descision and application process.

Note: I know the website is a little clunky, hopefully I can improve it in the future!
